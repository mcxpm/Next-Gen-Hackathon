MediWeave aims to pioneer the decentralisation of storing, recording, and retrieving medical data.

With a team of extremely dedicated individuals, we have created this concept using web3. Our goal is to alleviate inefficiencies in the medical sector, to make patient treatment a seamless and integrated process. Your data is split and stored as blocks that are linked together like a chain. Using the concept of blockweave, blocks in the chain are linked in such a way such that each block is only connected to two previous blocks and the next incoming block. The rest of the blocks get stored in various nodes across the network. When data needs to be retrieved, no matter how long ago it was added to the network, it can be retrieved almost instantly.

We understand that medical data is highly confidential and a private matter. Thus, it is our primary duty to ensure that your data is safe and secure. If you consent to our data sharing program where we share medical data with GOVERNMENT-APPROVED research agencies, for medical research, only aggregated data will be sent. This ensures that while your data stays protected, the medical field can take a strides forward in new solutions to solve medical problems and keep all of us in good health.

Future Plans

Overseas Expansion

As cyberattacks are frequent in every country, especially in less developed countries, there will be a gap that our product will address. Moreover, with low implementation and maintenance costs, scability of our solution will not be an issue.

Increase Services
